Interactive Fiction: Sun Wukong and the Journey to the West

Welcome to the world of Journey to the West! You will play as Sun Wukong (the Monkey King), helping Tang Sanzang go west to get sacred scriptures. Make choices along the way. Each choice changes your story and final ending.

Scene 1: Freeing the Sun Wukong

Tang Sanzang travels through a mountain called Five-Finger Mountain. He sees a monkey trapped under a big stone for 500 years. Tang Sanzang says a prayer, and a magic staff flies out. The monkey stands up and bows: “Master, I want to protect you on your journey!”

The monkey is Sun Wukong, once a divine official in Heavenly Court. Now he is free. They starts the jounery to the west.

However, a monster named Black Wind Demon has stolen the Buddha’s robe from a temple.

Scene 2: Fighting Alone

Wukong goes to the cave of Black Wind Demon. They fight three times, but the monster is too strong. Wukong runs away to his home, Flower-Fruit Mountain.

But the mountain is surrounded by heavenly soldiers. Wukong must choose what to do next.

Scene 3: Teamwork

Tang Sanzang leads Pigsy and Sandy to attack the cave at night. Pigsy uses his rake to open the path. Sandy guards behind. Wukong rushes into the cave.

But the demon has set a magic trap. All three are caught in a confusing fog and cannot escape.

Scene 4: Help from the Goddess

Guanyin arrives with a bottle of holy water and teaches Wukong how to break the trap. He succeeds and gets back the robe.

But the Black Wind Demon escapes and joins a stronger monster — the Yellow Robed Demon.

Scene 5: Monkey Army

Wukong gathers 100,000 monkeys and attacks the cave. The demons run away. The Black Wind Demon is captured.

But Wukong broke the rules of heaven. The Jade Emperor orders him to return his magic staff and face judgment.

Scene 6: Healing and Rest

Wukong uses a spell to stop the trap. The team escapes, but he is hurt badly.

Sandy wants to rest for three days. But Pigsy wants to eat meat and drink wine.

Scene 7: Holy Water

Guanyin pours holy water. The magic trap disappears. The team is safe and gets the robe back.

But the Black Wind Demon has escaped to the west and joined three powerful monsters at Lion Camel Mountain.

Scene 8: Attack the Monster Lair

The team enters Lion Camel Mountain. Three monsters attack. Wukong fights hard but is caught and locked in a dungeon.

Tang Sanzang is held prisoner. Pigsy and Sandy escape and must decide what to do.

Scene 9: Prepare the Team

They return to China. Tang Sanzang asks the emperor to send soldiers. An army comes, but they are lazy and cause trouble.

Wukong says: “We are monks on a holy mission. We should not depend on soldiers.”

Scene 10: Defy Heaven

Wukong refuses to obey heaven. He continues with Tang Sanzang. Heaven sends thunder gods to stop them.

To protect his master, Wukong breaks his golden headband and becomes a normal person.

Ending 1: Hero Falls

Wukong dies saving the mission. Tang Sanzang reaches the West and gets the scriptures. But without Wukong, the books are damaged. The true teachings are lost.

Type: Tragic Sacrifice

Scene 10: Obey Heaven

Wukong returns to heaven and gives back his staff. He loses freedom and cannot protect the master anymore.

Tang Sanzang travels alone and faces many dangers. He never reaches the West.

Ending 2: Mission Failed

Without Wukong, the journey ends halfway.Tang Sanzang dies in the desert. The scriptures are lost. The world stays dark.

Type: Failure & Regret

Scene 10: Keep Going

The team rests and heals. Wukong gets better. They continue their journey, fighting monsters and helping people.

Finally, they reach the Western Heaven. Buddha gives them the true scriptures. All four become enlightened.

Ending 3: Perfect Ending

They stay strong and never give up. After great hardship, they succeed. Tang Sanzang becomes a Buddha. Wukong becomes the Victorious Fighting Buddha.

Type: Success & Enlightenment

Scene 10: Enjoy Life

The team drinks wine and eats meat. They relax and forget danger.

At night, monsters attack and kidnap Tang Sanzang. Wukong wakes up too late.

Ending 4: Lost in Pleasure

Because of laziness, the mission fails. Master Tang is killed. The scriptures are burned. The team is punished forever.

Type: Failure & Destruction

Scene 10: Brave Battle

The team attacks Lion Camel Mountain. Wukong fights the three monsters and wins. But he is badly hurt.

Before dying, he tells Pigsy: “If I die, you must protect the master.”

Ending 5: Hero Dies

Wukong dies, but the journey continues. Pigsy protects the master and finishes the mission. Wukong becomes a guardian spirit.

Type: Brave Legacy

Scene 10: Army Protection

Heaven sends 100,000 soldiers to protect the team. Monsters fear them and stay away.

But the soldiers are proud and hurt innocent people. The people hate them.

Ending 6: Power Trap

They reach the West safely, but the scriptures are polluted by power. Master Tang becomes a “heavenly monk” but cannot help others.

Type: Compromise & Corruption

Scene 10: Smart Rescue

Pigsy and Sandy sneak into the dungeon. They steal the key and save Tang Sanzang. Wukong wakes up and defeats the monsters.

The team reunites and continues. They finally reach the West.

Ending 7: Victory by Wisdom

They win not by strength, but by cleverness and teamwork. The journey shines with human kindness. True Buddhism spreads.

Type: Wisdom & Light

Scene 10: Fight to the End

Pigsy and Sandy rush into the dungeon but are trapped. Wukong wakes up and fights all three monsters alone.

He wins, but Pigsy is hurt and Sandy disappears.

Ending 8: Victory with Pain

The mission is complete, but friends are lost. Tang Sanzang is sad. The scriptures are written in blood. The journey is full of sorrow.

Type: Sad Victory

Scene 10: Travel Light

They leave the army and travel only with the four monks. They face dangers, but trust each other and survive.

They reach the West and meet Buddha. They learn the truth of life.

Ending 9: Pure Journey

They rely only on belief and courage. The journey becomes a spiritual trip. All learn “no self.” True Buddhism spreads to the world.

Type: Awakening & Freedom

Scene 10: Split Army

The army moves in groups. But they argue and fight among themselves. Monsters attack when they are weak.

Tang Sanzang is kidnapped. Wukong kills traitors, but the scriptures are lost.

Ending 10: Broken Team

Power struggles destroy the team. The mission fails completely. Tang Sanzang is imprisoned. The scriptures are gone. People say: “If hearts are not united, how can we reach enlightenment?”

Type: Chaos & Destruction
